# 🌊 EcoSeas — UX/UI Design Case Study

![EcoSeas App Design](cover.png)

## 📋 Project Overview

**EcoSeas** is a mobile application specialized in **exploring marine creatures and raising environmental awareness**. The project was developed from the initial idea all the way to the final UI design, ensuring the best possible user experience.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Marine biodiversity is under threat, yet most people lack accessible tools to learn about and connect with ocean life. EcoSeas bridges this gap by providing an engaging, educational, and visually immersive experience for ocean enthusiasts and environmental advocates.

---

## 🔍 UX Process — Design Thinking Methodology

### 1️⃣ User Persona
- Studied the **target audience**, their needs, goals, and pain points
- Ensured the app aligns perfectly with user expectations and behaviors

### 2️⃣ Empathy Map
- Analyzed users' feelings and thoughts:
  - What do they **think & feel**?
  - What do they **see & hear**?
  - What do they **say & do**?
- Used insights to improve interaction quality and emotional engagement

### 3️⃣ Wireframes (Low & High Fidelity)
- Built **low-fidelity wireframes** to outline the basic structure and layout
- Developed **high-fidelity wireframes** to map the complete **User Flow**:
  - Registration & Onboarding
  - Marine creature exploration
  - Chat & community features
  - Environmental awareness content

### 4️⃣ UI Design
- Crafted a **visual identity** that reflects the spirit of the ocean and environmental conservation
- Applied a cohesive color palette, typography, and iconography inspired by marine life
